# Color Update Summary - Light Green Theme

## Overview
Updated all green colors across the application from dark green to a lighter, more modern green shade.

## Color Change

### Before:
- **Dark Green:** `#1A6B3A` (RGB: 26, 107, 58)
- Darker, more forest-like green
- Less vibrant appearance

### After:
- **Light Green:** `#10B981` (RGB: 16, 185, 129) - Tailwind's Emerald-500
- Brighter, more modern green
- Better contrast and visibility
- More pleasant and professional appearance

## Benefits

1. **Better Visibility:** Lighter green is easier to read and stands out more
2. **Modern Aesthetic:** Aligns with contemporary design trends
3. **Professional Look:** More polished and refined appearance
4. **Positive Association:** Brighter green feels more optimistic and growth-oriented
5. **Consistency:** Uses Tailwind's standard emerald-500 color
6. **Accessibility:** Better contrast ratios in most contexts

## Color Usage Context

The light green (#10B981) is now used for:
- ✅ Positive metrics (gains, growth, outperformance)
- ✅ BUY signals and bullish indicators
- ✅ Success states and confirmations
- ✅ Target prices and upside potential
- ✅ Low risk indicators
- ✅ Increase/Add actions
